Privacy Policy

Custom Aim: Aim Trainer — Last updated March 23, 2026

Omar Altamimi ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Custom Aim: Aim Trainer application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App does not require account creation, sign-in, or any form of user registration. However, our third-party advertising partner may collect certain data as described in Section 4.

2. Locally Stored Data

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device using standard iOS local storage (UserDefaults):

  • Game settings (sensitivity, sound preferences, haptic feedback)
  • Game scores, statistics, and progression data
  • Crosshair configuration and customization preferences
  • Onboarding selections (game preferences, rank, goals)

This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. It remains entirely on your device and under your control. You may delete this data at any time by using the "Reset All Stats" option within the App or by uninstalling the App.

3. Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. The App is free to download and use, and advertising supports its continued development.

To serve ads, Google AdMob may collect and process the following data:

  • Device identifiers (Identifier for Advertisers, or IDFA, if you grant permission)
  • IP address (used for approximate location to serve relevant ads)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, and conversions)
  • Device information (model, operating system version, screen size)

When you first launch the App, you will be asked for consent to personalized advertising via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized. In the European Economic Area (EEA), you may also be presented with a consent form under the GDPR.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for serving advertisements. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

We do not use analytics platforms, crash reporting tools, or any other third-party SDKs beyond the advertising service listed above.

5. Analytics and Tracking

The App uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework to request your permission before allowing Google AdMob to access your device's advertising identifier. You may change your tracking preferences at any time in your device's Settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 9+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Ads served to users are configured as non-child-directed. If you believe a child has been served inappropriate content, please contact us.

7. Data Security

All game data remains on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security features. Advertising data collected by Google AdMob is governed by Google's security practices and privacy policy.

8. Your Rights

You may clear all locally stored game data at any time through the App's settings. You can control advertising personalization through Apple's ATT settings on your device. Users in the EEA have additional rights under the GDPR, including the right to withdraw consent at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
