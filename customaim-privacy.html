Privacy Policy

Custom Aim: Aim Trainer — Last updated March 14, 2026

Omar Altamimi ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Custom Aim: Aim Trainer application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App does not require account creation, sign-in, or any form of user registration.

2. Locally Stored Data

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device using standard iOS local storage (UserDefaults):

  • Game settings (sensitivity, sound preferences, haptic feedback)
  • Game scores, statistics, and progression data
  • Crosshair configuration and customization preferences

This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. It remains entirely on your device and under your control. You may delete this data at any time by using the "Reset All Stats" option within the App or by uninstalling the App.

3. Network Communications

The App does not make any network requests or connect to the internet during normal use. All game assets, including crosshair images, maps, and sounds, are bundled within the App and do not require downloading.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party software development kits (SDKs), analytics platforms, advertising networks, or tracking technologies. We do not share any data with third parties.

5. Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework because no tracking occurs. We do not collect device identifiers (IDFA), usage analytics, crash reports, or any form of telemetry data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 9+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any data, no special provisions under the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) or similar regulations are required.

7. Data Security

Because we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of data breach or unauthorized access to your information through our services. All game data remains on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security features.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete from our systems. You may clear all locally stored game data at any time through the App's settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
