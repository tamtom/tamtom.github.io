Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I change my crosshair?
    Go to the Loadout tab from the home screen. Browse Free and Pro crosshairs, tap to select, and use the Editor to customize size, color, rotation, and opacity.
  • How do I reset my stats?
    Go to Config (gear icon) and tap "Reset All Stats" at the bottom.
  • Does the app work on iPad?
    Yes. Custom Aim is optimized for both iPhone and iPad in landscape mode.
  • Does the app collect my data?
    No. All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
  • The app is not responding or crashes.
    Try force-closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you are running the latest version from the App Store. You can also try deleting and reinstalling the app.
