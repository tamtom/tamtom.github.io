FAQ

How does Steps Share track my steps?

On iOS we use HealthKit and on Android we use Health Connect / device sensors, with your permission. You can revoke access at any time in your phone settings.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly share it in a room. We don’t sell personal data or show ads.

Can I export or delete my data?

Absolutely. You can export your steps and delete your account data at any time from Settings.
